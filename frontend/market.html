Market board

Recent moves that change the next event

Confirmed moves are separated from market pressure. Salary claims are avoided unless a reliable public source exists.

Free agent

sdy exits ENCE

Viktor "sdy" Orudzhev became one of the most notable available names after parting ways with ENCE on June 30.

AWP market

G2 move for r1nkle

G2 brought in r1nkle from Ninjas in Pyjamas while SunPayus moved to the bench, putting the AWP role back under the spotlight.

Rebuild

FaZe bench broky

FaZe's reset continued with broky benched and JBOEN added from BIG Academy, following a wider leadership shake-up.

Brazil

MIBR add nqz

Lucas "nqz" Soares moved from paiN to MIBR, giving the domestic Brazilian market a meaningful AWP transfer.

Asia

Senzu joins BC.Game

The MongolZ lost Senzu to BC.Game, a notable regional move involving a Major-level name from Asia's strongest core.

Veteran move

KRIMZ to EYEBALLERS

Freddy "KRIMZ" Johansson left fnatic's bench for EYEBALLERS, keeping a high-recognition Swedish veteran active.

Superteam

Falcons add karrigan

Falcons added karrigan as in-game leader, giving a star-heavy project clearer structure around NiKo and m0NESY.

Value note

Salary claims stay out

No public source is strong enough to name the highest-paid CS2 player here. Use performance, role and transfer demand instead.

Player value

Value is role fit plus timing

The strongest market profiles are not only rating leaders. Teams pay attention to AWP fit, IGL scarcity, anchor reliability, language fit and how quickly a player upgrades the next event cycle.

Rumor discipline

Confirmed first, rumors labeled

The page should not treat speculation as fact. Possible moves can be added later, but only with clear wording and a dated source.
