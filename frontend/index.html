ABOUT VELO / ARENA

Built to keep Counter-Strike 2 in focus.

VELO is an independent editorial arena shaped around the teams, tournaments, players and movement that define competitive CS2.

Origin

Founded by Gabriel Carvalho

Established in 2022 as a focused space for following the modern Counter-Strike scene.

Purpose

One arena for the competitive story

VELO brings teams, tournaments, players, roster movement and market context into one clear destination.

Project context

From scattered updates to useful perspective
